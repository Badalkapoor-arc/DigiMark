About Us

Our Mission

To simplify attendance management through biometric technology that ensures accuracy, transparency, and security.

Our Story

The idea for BioTrack was born when our team noticed how much time teachers, professors, and HR managers spent every day just taking attendance. We wanted to solve this problem with an automated system that saves time and ensures accuracy. What started as a small project is now a scalable solution used by multiple institutions.

Our Vision

    A smarter future where:
  • Students never lose attendance marks due to errors.
  • Teachers and professors save hours every month.
  • Organizations get real-time reports without manual calculations.
  • Technology makes learning and working environments more transparent.

Why Choose Us?

  • Accuracy You Can Trust – Every entry is authenticated with biometrics.
  • Saves Time – Attendance in seconds, not minutes.
  • User-Friendly – Simple interface for staff, students, and admins.
  • Data Security – Fingerprint data is encrypted and protected.
  • Customizable – Flexible enough to adapt to your institution’s needs.
